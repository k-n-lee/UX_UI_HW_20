My Skills

Conducting User Research

I am extremely curious about patterns in human behavior and how they impact design.

During the research process, I develop a deep understanding of key user needs and emotions by posing questions that spark reflection, uncover insights, and generate actionable information.

Brainstorming Solutions

Drawing on insights from user research, I focus on identifying features that offer creative, yet streamlined solutions to user challenges.

I also consider how users will interact with potential features and anticipate other ways to improve their experience as a result.

Wireframing and Prototyping

I love the process of translating abstract solutions into static and interactive visuals that bring designs to life.

I strive to create consistent, cohesive experiences within the designs while also considering the broader contexts in which the designs are being used.
